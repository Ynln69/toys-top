Privacy Policy

At Toys Pop, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to: - Provide and improve the game experience.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners for the following purposes: - Payment processing and fraud prevention.
